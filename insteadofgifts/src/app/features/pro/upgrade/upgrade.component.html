Questions

When will I be charged?
You'll be charged $9.99 today and on the same day each month. The subscription is billed via Stripe — your card details are never stored on our servers.
Can I cancel at any time?
Yes. Cancel from your account settings and you'll keep Pro access until the end of the current billing period. No questions asked.
What happens to my campaigns if I downgrade?
Your existing campaigns stay active. Pro features (cover photos, custom messages, QR codes) will be hidden for contributors but the data is preserved so you can re-enable them if you resubscribe.
Is there a free trial?
Not right now, but the Free plan is genuinely fully featured for running a campaign. Pro is for people who run campaigns regularly and want that extra polish.
